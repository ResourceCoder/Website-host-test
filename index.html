What makes our concerete posts so permanent?

Maintenance Free

No need for continual painting, treatment or recoating. No labor costs associated with constant maintenance.

Theft Proof

Continual replacement due to constant theft makes fencing expensive. Rising steel prices make scrap metal more attractive and durable.

Fire Proof

Annual fire hazards are a reality in agriculture. Timber post CANNOT outlast Concrete. Ongoing post treatment is labour intensive and is still no cure for runaway fires

Simple Installation

Posts are planted 0.6m deep using pick and shovel, petrol driven auger or hydraulic rammer. Soil type would determine the hole size.

A combination of high tensile spring steel, prestressed concrete technology and specialised admixtures has delivered an excellent solution to fence posts. Our concrete posts are designed to outlast all other conventional fence posts. This is why we call them permanent posts.

FUNCTIONAL AND AESTHETIC NOTCHES

The specific design of the 10mm notches spaced at 100mm apart does not interfere with the strength integrity of the post, and allows grip and tie-downs onto the post as a clear guidance for the installer. Slippage happens as an animal or person climbs through the fence and moves the wire strands, allowing future passage.
